<b>Help for the bot</b>

COMMAND DESCRIPTION:
•<code>/start</code>: provides a nice introduction—I hope!
•<code>/url</code>: get the download link of the video
•<code>/download</code>: download the video directly; not recommended for large video files, especially large YouTube videos. 
•<code>/dl</code>: alias for <code>/download</code> 
•<code>/help</code>: display this message. 

COMMAND SYNTAX:
•<code>/start</code>

•<code>/url [video_url]</code>
   e.g.: <code>/url https://example.com/</code> 

•<code>/download [video_url]</code>
   e.g.: <code>/download https://example.com/</code> 

•<code>/dl [video_url]</code>
   e.g.: <code>/dl https://example.com/</code> 

•<code>/help</code>
